Welcome to VENOLK1

Your complete study management platform — books, notes, spaced repetition, prayer times, Pomodoro, mind maps and more, all in one app.

📚

Track Your Books

Add books and chapters. Complete 4 stages per chapter: Reading → MCQ → CQ → Exam. Finishing all 4 automatically adds it to Spaced Repetition.

⏱

Focus with Pomodoro

Work in focused 25-minute bursts. Your phone will vibrate and play a sound when time is up. Link sessions to subjects for detailed study analytics.

🧠

Spaced Repetition

The SM-2 algorithm schedules your chapter and flashcard reviews at the optimal time — harder items appear more often, mastered items less often.

🕌

Prayer & Routine

The Prayer Chamber calculates accurate Salah times for your city. Use the Weekly Routine planner to build a consistent study schedule around your prayers.
